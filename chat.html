Frequently Asked Questions

What can I ask the chatbot?

You can ask about NGOs by category (education, healthcare, etc.), location (any district in Tamil Nadu), how to volunteer, donation information, and specific organization details.

Can I chat in Tamil?

Yes! Our chatbot understands both Tamil and English. Feel free to communicate in whichever language you're most comfortable with.

How do I find NGOs in my district?

Simply mention your district name in your message. For example: "Show me NGOs in Coimbatore" or "Healthcare organizations in Madurai".

Are all NGOs verified?

Yes! All NGOs in our database are thoroughly verified for authenticity and credibility. We ensure all contact information and details are accurate and up-to-date.

How can I volunteer or donate?

Ask the chatbot "How can I volunteer?" or "How to donate?" and it will provide you with detailed information and direct contact details for the NGOs you're interested in.

Is my conversation private?

Absolutely! Your conversations are completely private and secure. We don't store or share any personal information from your chats.
